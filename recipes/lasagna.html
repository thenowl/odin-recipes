Lasagna Recipe

The world's best Lasagna

Learn how to cook the world's best lasagna

Ingredients:

Ingredients for the bolognaise:

  • olive oil
  • 2 onions
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 carrots
  • quarter of a celery
  • 2 gloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp. tomato paste
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 2 cups of broth
  • 2 cups of chopped tomatoes
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • oregano to taste
  • basil to taste

Ingredients for the bechamel:

  • 3 tbsp. butter
  • 3 tbsp. flour
  • 2 cups of milk
  • nutmeg to taste
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste

Ingredients for the lasagna:

  • several lasagne sheets
  • parmesan cheese
  • mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Peel the onions, carrots and celery, cut into small cubes and place in a bowl. Peel the garlic and chop finely.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a wide pan. Lightly sauté the onions, carrots and celery.
  3. Add the ground beef and fry until brown and crumbly.
  4. Finally, add the garlic and tomato paste and fry everything lightly.
  5. Add broth and tomatoes. Season with pepper, oregano, and basil. Stir everything carefully, then cover and simmer for at least 20 - 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Finally season with salt, stir and turn off the oven.
  6. For the béchamel sauce, melt the butter in a small saucepan. Add salt, pepper and freshly grated nutmeg. Sprinkle with the flour and stir with a whisk. Slowly pour in the milk while stirring and bring to the boil briefly. If the sauce becomes too thick, add more milk.
  7. Preheat the oven to 390 °C fan.
  8. Put some of the bolognaise sauce in a wide ovenproof casserole dish and spread it out so that the bottom is covered. Cover with lasagne sheets and layer them alternately. Pour some béchamel sauce on each layer and put most of it on top of the last layer of lasagne sheets. Sprinkle with Parmesan or mozzarella cubes and finish with a little freshly ground pepper.
  9. Bake the lasagne in the oven for 30 - 40 minutes. If you like, turn on the grill for the last 5 minutes so that it becomes browner and crispier on top.
  10. Serve fresh and hot!

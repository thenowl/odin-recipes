Szegediner Goulash Recipe

A spicy Hungarian tradition

This is an easy recipe for a Hungarian classic

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork goulash, lean
  • 30g lard or frying oil
  • 30g flour
  • 300g onions
  • 1 tbsp. salt
  • half a tbsp. pepper
  • 1 tbsp. cumin
  • 2 tbsp. paprika powder
  • 70g tomato paste
  • 2 garlic gloves, pressed
  • 200ml broth
  • 300g sauerkraut
  • sour creme
  • yogurt if meat is very lean

Steps

  1. Fry the goulash in hot lard until it's nice and brown.
  2. Dust with flour, add onions, salt, pepper, caraway, paprika, tomato paste (not tomatoes!) and the pressed garlic cloves.
    (note: the flour makes the frying mixture a bit mushy, don't worry, it will disappear completely and it will be very creamy, just as the goulash should be)
  3. When the flour is light brown, top up with vegetable stock.
  4. If the meat is very lean, add another 150 ml of yogurt!
  5. Braise well, approx. 20 - 30 minutes.
  6. Add the sauerkraut, stir well and braise for another 30 minutes.
  7. To serve, add a little sour cream to the goulash.

Tarte Flambée Recipe

A Alsatian specialty and classic

This is an easy recipe for a classic Alsatian Tarte Flambée

Ingredients:

  • 440g flour
  • 200ml water, lukewarm
  • 2 egg yolk
  • 6 tbsp. oil
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • some flour for the work surface
  • some oil for brushing
  • sour creme
  • onions, cut in rings
  • bacon cubes

Steps

  1. First knead the ingredients together for five minutes with a hand mixer and then work them together briefly by hand.
  2. Then brush with a little oil and leave to rest wrapped in foil for about 30 minutes.
  3. Roll out thinly on a lightly floured work surface and place on baking paper.
  4. Place on a baking tray, spread sour creme on the surface and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  5. Top with onion rings and bacon cubes.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 430 degrees, bottom rack, for about 10 - 15 minutes.
